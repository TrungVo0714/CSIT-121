Basic Principles of Ballhandling and Dribbling

  1. When handling the basketball, always have your head up, and vision of the court. You want your focus to be on the opposite net.
  2. Keep your back straight while in your dribbling stance. Bad posture will force your head to come down, causing you to lose vision of the court.
  3. Be ball quick and dribble with your fingertips. You want to pound the ball on the fingertips of your hand so you can have a good feel for the basketball. You don’t want to dribble on the palms, or you can’t control the ball. You control the ball – don’t let the ball control you.
